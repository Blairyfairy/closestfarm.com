About ClosestFarm

Explore farmers markets near you. Check-in, upload photos, and share visits with or without logging in. Fully static demo with gamification and modern UI.

How to Use

• Optional login for username badges
• Search markets and click markers
• Check-in and upload photos
• Share visits to social media
• Earn badges even anonymously

Technology Used

• Leaflet.js + OpenStreetMap
• HTML / CSS / JavaScript
• LocalStorage for persistence
• Responsive, modern UI
